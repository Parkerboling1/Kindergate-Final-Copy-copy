Our Mission:
Our mission is to develop a positive, accepting, nurturing and challenging
learning environment for infant and preschool children.
We acomplish this by:

Providing trained caregivers who have demonstrated an ability to care for children in a responsive, sensitive and mature manner. Recognizing that cognitive, social, emotional, spiritual and physical growth emerge and develop at different times, rates of speed and through a variety of teaching methods influenced by personal history within individual cultural context. Appreciating the value of play as an important learning method for children. Providing well-defined structures so that children know exactly what is expected of them.
Kindergate's program is based upon Christian principles and endeavors to communicate them at a child's level. We aim to share the feeling that God is a loving Father who can be trusted to care for all of us. We believe that every child will benefit from this kind of environment. Our strength lies within our people. Our staff is committed to providing the best care possible for your child. All staff are certified in First Aid and CPR and participate in a minimum of 12 hours of in-service training annually. In addition, we require all staff to submit to drug testing and criminal history checks. We are a non-smoking facility. Staff and parents are not permitted to smoke on the premises.
